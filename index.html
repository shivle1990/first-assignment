Vision of "Bangladesh 2020"

Bangladesh envisions becoming a prosperous, inclusive, and developed nation by 2041. This vision focuses on eradicating poverty, ensuring equitable education, fostering innovation, and empowering youth. Priorities include sustainable economic growth, robust infrastructure, green energy, and climate resilience. Gender equity, technological advancements, and global trade integration drive progress. With strong governance and social harmony, Bangladesh aspires to secure a leading position in the global arena, ensuring a brighter future for all.

Recent News

Bangladesh's first satellite, **Bangabandhu-1**, was launched on **May 11, 2018**. The satellite was launched aboard a SpaceX Falcon 9 rocket from the Kennedy Space Center in Florida, USA. It marked a significant milestone for Bangladesh, making it the 57th country in the world to own a communications satellite.
